Privacy Policy

Terse — Token Optimizer for LLMs (Chrome Extension)
Last updated: April 9, 2026

All text optimization happens locally in your browser. Your prompts never leave your device.

What Terse Does

Terse is a browser extension that compresses AI prompts to save tokens and reduce costs. It reads text from AI chat interfaces (like ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini), optimizes it using local algorithms, and can write the optimized text back.

Data We Process

Text content: When you click "Capture" or use a keyboard shortcut, Terse reads the text from the active AI chat input on your current page. This text is processed entirely within your browser using a local optimization engine. No text is transmitted to any server.

Optimization statistics: Terse stores token counts (e.g., "saved 50 tokens") in your browser's local storage. These are aggregate numbers, not prompt content.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect, transmit, or store your prompt text
  • We do not track your browsing activity
  • We do not use analytics or tracking scripts
  • We do not sell any data to third parties
  • We do not use your data for advertising

Optional Account Features

If you choose to sign in (optional), we collect:

  • Email address — to identify your account
  • Account ID — for subscription verification

This information is sent to terseai.org only to verify your subscription status. No prompt content is ever transmitted.

Data Storage

All data is stored locally in your browser using Chrome's chrome.storage.local API:

  • User preferences (theme, optimization mode)
  • Aggregate optimization statistics (token counts)
  • Account credentials (if signed in)

You can clear all stored data by removing the extension or using Chrome's "Clear browsing data" feature.

Permissions

  • activeTab: Read text from the current page when you click Capture
  • storage: Save your preferences and statistics locally
  • clipboardWrite: Copy optimized text to your clipboard

Third-Party Services

The extension communicates with terseai.org only for optional account and subscription features. No third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used.

Children's Privacy

Terse is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as the extension evolves. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
